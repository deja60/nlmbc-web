Our Shepherd

Rev. Dr. Cardes H. Brown, Jr.

Senior Pastor

Reverend Dr. Cardes H. Brown, Jr. is a true visionary and leader. At nineteen years of age, during his sophomore year at North Carolina A&T State University, he accepted the calling of God to preach the gospel of Jesus Christ.

For over fifty years, he has served as the Senior Pastor of New Light Missionary Baptist Church. Under his leadership, the membership tripled, the Family Life Center was built, and countless community outreach programs were launched.
