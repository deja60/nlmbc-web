Your First Visit

What to Expect

🚗

Parking

Free parking is available on the church campus at 1105 Willow Road, Greensboro, NC 27401. Our parking team will be happy to assist you.

👕

What to Wear

Come as you are! There is no dress code. Some members dress formally, others casually — all are welcome exactly as they are.

🎵

Worship Style

Our services feature powerful praise & worship music, heartfelt prayer, and an anointed message from God's Word. Services typically run 60–90 minutes.

👋

The Welcome

First-time visitors are welcomed during our services. Our greeters and ushers will make sure you feel at home from the moment you walk in.

🍼

Infants & Toddlers

Our Nursery is available during Sunday morning services for children ages 18 months to 4 years old, staffed by caring and trained volunteers.

🧒

Children

Children's Church meets the 1st, 2nd, and 4th Sundays at 11:00 AM in the Children's Church Building — a fun, safe environment for elementary-age children.

Grow in Faith

Christian Education

Our Christian Education ministry offers classes throughout the church calendar year that are biblical and basic; real and relevant in meeting the needs of the congregation to mature in their relationship with God, with others, and to become better followers of Christ.

We welcome and assist all to have an understanding of who they are as the children of God, through the transforming power of God's Word.
