Come Meet God

Worship & Services

There is a variety of services for worshippers of all ages at New Light. All week long, come, meet God, give thanks, and nourish your spirit.

Weekend

Sunday Worship Services

☀

Morning Worship

9:00 AM — Every Sunday

Join us in the Sanctuary for morning worship each Sunday. Services feature praise & worship, prayer, and an anointed message from God's Word. Both in-person and online attendance options are available.

📍 Sanctuary 🔴 Also available live online
📖

Sunday School

8:00 AM — Every Sunday

Our Sunday School classes provide biblical instruction for all ages. Grow in your knowledge of God's Word before the morning worship service.

👧

Children's Church

11:00 AM — 1st, 2nd & 4th Sundays

A safe, welcoming place where elementary school children learn about the love of God and the Word of God. Caring leaders use Bible stories, activities, and games to introduce children to God's wonder and His promises.

📍 Children's Church Building
🍼

Nursery

During Sunday Morning Services

Our nurturing nursery staff cares for your youngest children (ages 18 months – 4 years) so you can worship with peace of mind.

📍 Nursery Ages 18 months – 4 years

Midweek

MidWeek Manna

A great worship opportunity for those who missed Sunday and those who want a deeper relationship with God during the week.

🌙

Evening Service

Every Wednesday at 7:00 PM

Our 7pm midweek service offers a full worship experience with Bible teaching, prayer, and fellowship for the whole family.

Grow Deeper

Christian Education

Our Christian Education ministry offers classes throughout the church calendar year that are biblical and basic; real and relevant in meeting the needs of the congregation to mature in their relationship with God, with others, and to become better followers of Christ.

We welcome and assist all to have an understanding of who they are as the children of God, through the transforming power of God's Word.
