Rev. Dr. Cardes H. Brown, Jr.

Rev. Dr. Cardes H. Brown, Jr.

Senior Pastor · New Light Missionary Baptist Church

📞 336.273.5579 ext. 4 ✉ chbrown@nlmbc.com

Reverend Dr. Cardes H. Brown, Jr. is a true visionary and leader. As the son of the late Reverend Cardes H. Brown, Sr., and the late Mrs. Jocile Powell Brown, Dr. Brown was born in Rocky Mount, North Carolina, and attended Booker T. Washington High School. At nineteen years of age, during his sophomore year at North Carolina A&T State University, he accepted the calling of God to preach the gospel of Jesus Christ.

For over sixty years, Pastor Brown has devoted himself to pastoral ministry. Over the course of his ministerial career, he has been privileged to serve as pastor of various churches across North Carolina. For fifty years, he has served as the Senior Pastor of New Light Missionary Baptist Church in Greensboro, North Carolina — a tenure marked by tripling the church's membership and launching countless new ministries and community outreach programs.

Dr. Brown is a man of many hats, with a heart for social justice and a deep commitment to his community. Having served on numerous boards and with several organizations, such as the state and local NAACP, Pulpit Forum of Greensboro, and Maya Angelou Center for Health Equity. In 2019, as a testament to his unwavering commitment to justice, equity, and equality, Dr. Brown was the recipient of the Benjamin Elijah Mays Award.

Alongside him in ministry and life is the lovely Diane Pierce Brown. They are blessed with four children, six grandchildren, and two great-grandchildren.

Education

  • Graduate of Theology & Bachelor of Arts — Greensboro Bible College
  • Master of Divinity — Shaw Divinity School, Raleigh, NC
  • Doctor of Ministry / Worship Studies — Eastern Carolina Christian College
  • Honorary Doctor of Humane Letters — Apex School of Theology, Durham, NC
